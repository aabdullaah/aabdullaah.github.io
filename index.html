Md. Abdullah

Educator, Photographer and Blogger

Currently an undergraduate student of Department of History, University of Dhaka. I am so much passionate in History related sectors. I want to be a researcher in this field.

Apart from studies I am associated with some extra curricular activities. I was a cadet of BNCC Naval wing, University of Dhaka contingent. Currently i am serving Secondary and Intermediate Level Students Welfare Association - SILSWA as President which is working for the betterment of shcool, college and university level students of Bangladesh. I am also a Team Leader of Team Communication and Public Relations of Guidlines for Non-STEM Higher Study Aspirants - GNHSA which is working for higher study in abroad for the students of Bangladesh. Working with this clubs or organisations I have gathered a lot of knowledge such as Human Resource Management, Event Management, Logistics Management, Public Relation, Communication and IT etc.

I love to travel, watch movie. I dream one day i will go for world tour. Photography is my another passion. I take pictures to keep enliven the important moment of our life.

In a nutshell I am a person full of confidence and enthusiasm. I love to communicate with people, can speak fluently in both Bengali and English. I love to connect with people.
